Recipe for scrambled eggs

Here is a recipe for some delicious scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2 tbs cream

Instructions

  1. Melt butter in frying pan
  2. Gently mix eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter melts, add cream and eggs
  4. Mix eggs in pan using spatula
  5. Remove from heat, and place on a plater
